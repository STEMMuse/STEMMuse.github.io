Become a sponsor

You or your company can support women's professional success in STEM by sponsoring our 2023 program at UT Austin and nationally thereafter

Sponsors have the opportunity to give back to the community and to reach job candidates well before they step into your office or lab on day 1. Our sponsors have the opportunity to:

  • Provide your employees with opportunities to promote the professional success of women in STEM and to hone their leadership skills
  • Create sustained, meaningful interactions with potential future employees through mentor slots and a cohort of mentees devoted to your company
  • Host events with our network, including but not limited to: technical talks, resume review sessions/mock interviews, and company site tours
  • Host career booths
  • Post positions to our job board
  • Access program data and success metrics, enabling you to better understand your recruits and to reward your employees for their service
Contact us to be a founding customer!
