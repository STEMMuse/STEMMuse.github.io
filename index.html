We connect women in STEM higher education and industry through networking and mentorship to patch the leaky pipeline. We are STEM Muse.

A community and mentorship program that cultivates a network, creates career opportunities, and fosters a sense of belonging for women in STEM.

Our program is designed to meet our mentees’ needs, which starts with letting mentees select their mentors. We optimize that process with an algorithm so that as many get their top choice as possible. Our program matches academic mentees who are undergraduate students, graduate students, postdoctoral associates and research staff with more advanced academic and industry mentors and enables flexible scheduling among mentorship pairs. We provide structure with in-person and virtual group programming and monthly themed conversation guides to facilitate productive conversations.

Our tailored mentorship program enhances the recruitment, retention, & success of women in STEM.

Recruitment

We bridge the gap between STEM higher education and careers. Our mentees have been hired as interns and even full-time to mentors’ companies.

Retention

We help women stay in the positions they earn and excel in them. Sustained interactions and exposure to companies and women at those companies through our program and network improves the assimilation of new employees.

Success

Both mentees and mentors report an enhanced sense of belonging and concrete academic, professional, and career achievements.
