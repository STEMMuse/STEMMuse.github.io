We connect women in STEM higher education and industry through networking and mentorship to patch the leaky pipeline.

Our platform and program are designed to meet our menteesâ€™ needs, which starts with letting mentees select their mentors. We optimize that process with an algorithm so that as many get their top choice as possible. Our program matches academic mentees who are undergraduate students, graduate students, postdoctoral associates and research staff with more advanced academic and industry mentors and enables flexible scheduling among mentorship pairs. We provide structure with in-person and virtual group programming and monthly themed conversation guides to facilitate productive conversations.

Learn More

Our mission is to enhance the recruitment, retention, & success of women in STEM.

Recruitment

We bridge the gap between STEM higher education and careers. Our mentees have been hired for internships and full-time positions at mentors' companies.

Retention

We help women stay in the positions they earn and excel in them. Sustained exposure to companies through our program and network improves the assimilation of new employees.

Success

We equip our mentees and mentors with soft skills including professional communication, management, and leadership. Both mentees and mentors report an enhanced sense of belonging and concrete academic, professional, and career achievements.

We are a community and a mentorship program that creates career opportunities and fosters a sense of belonging for women in STEM.
